Why did I create this site?

The image on these pages are my own. I wanted to share part of the reason why I chose to go with these machines. The specs were a big part of it, the other part is that they are modular. These machines, unlike all the other comparable machines, don't have screens. They don't hold your designs. Your computer does all this. Using your computer in this way allows you to be modular. One computer can control up to 30 machines. This allows you to run all of your machines on the same project at once (like a multihead) or different projects on each.
